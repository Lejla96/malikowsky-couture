About Malikowsky

Muamed Malikovski — known to the world as Malikowsky — is the only Romani haute couturier in North Macedonia. Born in Bitola into an artisan family, he showed a passion for art from childhood but was steered toward law, achieving top marks before making the defining choice to follow his true calling.

“Everyone around me expected me to be someone that wasn't me, someone I've never met in my soul. I knew it — I decided to listen to my heart and follow my dreams.”

Without family support and constantly told “you can't do it,” Muamed enrolled at the Faculty of Fashion Design at Goce Delchev University in Stip. He created his very first design at age 16. His breakthrough came with support from ERIAC (European Roma Institute for Arts and Culture) and the EU-funded Roma Digital Boost programme, which helped him transform passion into a globally recognized brand.

His “Aurora” design won 2nd place in the Miss Freedom of the World competition; another creation was named Best National Costume at Miss Summer 2021 in Albania. Today his gowns are worn by clients across Macedonia, Serbia, Germany, Switzerland, Belgium, France, England, Sweden, and as far as Melbourne, Australia. His greatest inspirations are Baroque luxury, Oriental culture from his Muslim roots, and the rich artistic heritage of Roma folklore and tradition.

“No matter how discouraging the circumstances, there is always a path to success. Even if it seems impossible to others — let’s create stories worth telling loudly and proudly.”
